Is Your Water Pump Working Hard — or Wasting Your Power and Water Every Day?

What makes you happy everyday?

At 360 Irrigation Hub, we provide premium-grade water pumps engineered for performance, efficiency, and durability. Whether for domestic use, commercial projects, agricultural irrigation, industrial applications, or borehole systems, our pumps are carefully selected to deliver consistent water flow and optimal pressure — no matter the demand or terrain.

We don’t just sell pumps — we design complete water movement solutions tailored to your needs, ensuring smooth installation, power efficiency, and long-term reliability.

Power your water systems with reliable, high-performance pumps from 360 Irrigation Hub. We supply and install top brands for homes, farms, industries, and boreholes — ensuring efficient flow, steady pressure, and long-lasting performance.

1. Submersible Borehole Pumps

Overview: Fully sealed pumps designed to operate submerged, delivering high lift and steady performance for deep water extraction.

Common Uses: Drawing clean water from deep boreholes and wells; supplying farms, homes and industries; high-pressure irrigation systems.

Advantages: Energy-efficient, quiet operation, minimal maintenance, rust-resistant construction and reliable high-lift performance.

2. Surface Centrifugal Pumps

Overview: Centrifugal pumps mounted above ground that move large volumes of water efficiently for shallow sources.

Common Uses: Farm irrigation and sprinkler systems, tank-to-tank transfers, domestic and light commercial water supply.

Advantages: Easy installation and servicing, strong pressure for medium-distance transfer, and compatibility with solar or electric power sources.

3. Solar-Powered Water Pumps

Overview: Environmentally-friendly pumps that convert sunlight into pumping power — ideal for off-grid and remote locations.

Common Uses: Remote farms and rural water projects, livestock watering, community boreholes and sustainable irrigation systems.

Advantages: Zero fuel cost, low operational expenses, suitability for areas without reliable grid power, and smart controllers with dry-run protection.

4. Booster Pumps

Overview: Compact pressure-raising units designed to ensure consistent flow and pressure across properties and multi-outlet systems.

Common Uses: Domestic pressure boosting, commercial buildings and hotels, and irrigation networks that require constant pressure.

Advantages: Automatic pressure control, elimination of inconsistent flow, compact footprint and improved performance across multiple outlets.

5. Industrial & Multistage Pumps

Overview: Heavy-duty multistage pumps engineered for high-pressure, long-distance water delivery and continuous operation in demanding environments.

Common Uses: Industrial processing plants, complex irrigation networks, high-rise building supply and municipal systems.

Advantages: High-pressure output, stable continuous performance, durable corrosion-resistant construction, and suitability for intensive duty cycles.

6. Drainage & Sewage Pumps

Overview: Robust pumps designed to handle wastewater, solids and flood dewatering tasks safely and reliably.

Common Uses: Flood drainage, sewage management, construction site dewatering and wastewater transfer for commercial and residential sites.

Advantages: Heavy-duty impellers for solid handling, corrosion-resistant materials, submersible options and automatic float-switch operation for hands-free control.
