Is Your Borehole Delivering Less Water Than It Used To — And You’re Not Sure Why?

What makes you happy everyday?

Restore your water flow, pressure, and quality with expert borehole rehabilitation by 360 Irrigation Hub. We specialize in diagnosing and reviving underperforming or non-functional boreholes — ensuring they deliver clean, consistent water once again.

Don’t drill a new borehole when your old one can flow again.” At 360 Irrigation Hub, we specialize in reviving boreholes back to full performance through expert inspection, cleaning, and rehabilitation — saving you time, money, and the environment.

Borehole Inspection & Diagnosis

We use advanced equipment to identify blockages, pump failures, collapsed casings, or declining yields — pinpointing the exact cause of poor performance before recommending a solution.

Cleaning & De-Silting

Our team performs high-pressure cleaning, de-silting, and disinfection to remove sand, mud, and bacteria buildup, restoring optimal water clarity and flow rate.

Pump Testing & Replacement

We test pump efficiency and replace worn-out or undersized units with high-performance submersible pumps for improved water delivery and energy efficiency.

Borehole Re-Casing & Rehabilitation

We repair or reline damaged borehole casings, seal cracks, and reinforce structures to extend the borehole’s lifespan and prevent contamination or collapse.

Water Quality Testing

We conduct detailed chemical and bacterial analysis to ensure your borehole water meets safety standards for domestic, commercial, or agricultural use.

Preventive Maintenance Plans

Keep your borehole performing optimally year-round with scheduled maintenance programs that include regular cleaning, inspections, and performance monitoring.
